Getting the Flowgram bookmarklets is a smart move!

You'll be able to add pages as you browse. No more copying and pasting URLs! You'll also be able to add a wider range of pages.

  1. Clicking the Flowgram Maker bookmarklet brings up the Flowgram Maker right away.
  2. Clicking the Quick Add bookmarklet adds the page your browser is currently on. The next time you launch the Flowgram Maker your new page will be there.
