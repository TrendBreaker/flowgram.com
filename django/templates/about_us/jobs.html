Web Producer

Position Details

The Flowgram Producer is responsible for a wide range of duties that span customer service, testing, customer acquisition, PR and community management. The producer needs to combine a passion for Flowgram's success with great communication skills, a genuine concern for customer satisfaction and tech savviness.

Responsibilities

  • Work with the rest of the Flowgram team to test new features and functionality
  • Provide customer support to Flowgram users and work with team to prioritize and resolve any issues
  • Participate and help manage the Flowgram community
  • Provide VIP support to select Flowgram partners
  • Track and report on Blog/Press Flowgram mentions
  • Participate in outward bound marketing targetting bloggers and potential Flowgrammers
  • Make demo Flowgrams

Experience & Skills

  • Very strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Deep Windows/Mac skills for trouble shooting users' technical issues
  • History of participating in a number of online communities and social networks
  • Experience creating user generated content

About Us

Flowgram is building a new multimedia-empowered way to communicate. The Flowgram product has captured the imagination of a prestigious group of individuals with exceptional track records including Abhay Parekh (founder), who co-founded Fast Forward Networks which sold for over $1b. Abhay is also venture partner at Accel Capital and an Adjunct Professor of EECS at UC Berkeley. Flowgram investors include Reid Hoffman (Paypal, Linkedin), the founders of Flickr and Half.com, the founding CEO of Macromedia, and members of the founding team of WebEx and Tibco Software.

Blending industry veterans with promising talent, Flowgram prides itself on the quality of our team. We believe that a small group of passionate, smart, fun people can achieve amazing things when given the right focus and environment.
